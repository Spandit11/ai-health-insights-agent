# 🔮 Future Enhancements

This document outlines the planned roadmap for the AI Health Insights Agent platform.

The goal is to evolve the system from a healthcare report analysis Proof of Concept into a production-grade Agentic AI healthcare assistant.

---

# 🚀 Phase 7 - Enhanced Health Intelligence

## Confidence Scoring

Introduce confidence scores for:

* Metric extraction
* Risk assessment
* Recommendations
* Chat responses

Example:

```text
HbA1c Status: Pre-Diabetic

Confidence Score: 98%
```

Benefits:

* Increased trust
* Better explainability
* Improved user experience

---

## Advanced Medical Knowledge Base

Introduce curated medical reference data:

* Diabetes guidelines
* Cholesterol guidelines
* Blood pressure ranges
* Preventive health recommendations

Benefits:

* More reliable recommendations
* Reduced hallucinations
* Better clinical reasoning

## Multi-Report Comparison

Allow users to upload multiple reports.

Example:

```text

     ↓
```

* Cholesterol trend tracking
